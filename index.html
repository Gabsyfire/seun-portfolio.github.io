About Me

I'm Seun Gabriel Ogunwande, a Power Platform Developer and Business Solutions Architect specializing in transforming business processes through intelligent automation and modern cloud solutions.

With deep expertise in Microsoft Power Platform, Dynamics 365 CRM, and Azure, I design and build scalable solutions that bridge the gap between business needs and technical implementation. My work focuses on creating efficient workflows, integrating complex systems, and empowering organizations to do more with less.

Beyond development, I bring a strategic perspective on IT service management, cybersecurity culture, and AI adoption. I believe in building trust-based security frameworks and helping organizations navigate the intersection of emerging technology and practical business operations.

I also bring hands-on DevOps expertise, managing CI/CD pipelines, Azure infrastructure, and automated deployment workflows for enterprise environments. From solution deployments across multiple environments to infrastructure as code, I ensure reliable and repeatable delivery of business-critical systems.
