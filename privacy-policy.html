Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 February 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how gabsyfy.co.uk ("this website") uses cookies and collects data when you visit. I respect your privacy and am committed to being transparent about the data I collect and how it is used.

What Cookies Are Used

This website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site. Google Analytics sets the following cookies:

Cookie Purpose Duration
_ga Distinguishes unique visitors 2 years
_gid Distinguishes unique visitors 24 hours
_gat Limits the rate of data collection 1 minute

Additionally, a cookieConsent value is stored in your browser's local storage to remember your cookie preference. This is not a cookie and does not track you in any way.

What Data Is Collected

When you accept cookies, Google Analytics collects the following anonymised data:

  • Pages you visit and how long you spend on each page
  • How you arrived at the website (e.g. search engine, direct link)
  • Your device type, browser, and operating system
  • Your approximate geographic location (country/city level only)
  • Session duration and interactions with the site

How Your Data Is Anonymised

IP anonymisation is enabled on this website. This means your full IP address is never stored by Google Analytics. Instead, your IP address is truncated before it is sent to Google's servers, ensuring that no personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

No personal data such as your name, email address, or any information you submit through the contact form is collected by Google Analytics.

Your Choices

When you first visit this website, a cookie consent banner will appear at the bottom of the page. You have two options:

  • Accept — Google Analytics cookies will be set and anonymous usage data will be collected to help improve this website.
  • Decline — No analytics cookies will be set and no usage data will be collected. The website will function normally.

Your choice is saved in your browser so you will not be asked again. If you would like to change your preference, click the button below:

Preferences cleared. The consent banner will appear on your next page load.

Third-Party Services

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics uses cookies to help analyse how visitors use this website. The information generated by cookies is transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States.

Google may use this data to contextualise and personalise ads within its own advertising network. For more information on how Google handles your data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

You can also opt out of Google Analytics across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or would like to request the deletion of any data associated with your visit, please get in touch.
